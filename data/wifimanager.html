ESP Wi-Fi Manager

Instructions

  1. Enter the SSID and password of the network you want to connect to.
  2. Enter the IP address of the router device in this network.
  3. Enter the IP address of the client device (ESP).
  4. Enter the IP address of the host device running Node-red.
  5. Click "Submit" to save the configuration.
  6. The ESP will reboot and connect to the network.
